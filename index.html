  • Scott Pilgrim vs. the World

    • Scott Pilgrim is a volume of stories about a twenty-two year old. He has not hit reality. he falls in love with Ramona flowers, but in order to date her he must defeat her sven evil exes. Does he win and will he hit reality? Read it to find out.

  • The Wake

    • The Wake is a story that is set in the present and future. It involves the destruction of the world from an unknown species of underwater dwelling "mermaids." They consume and can telepathically link to humans and creat illusions. The first few stories are set in the present and the last stories are set in the future where the earth has become a water world.

  • Batman End Game.

    • Batman Endgame is the latest chapter that involves the return of the joker. The Joker may or may not know the true identity of Batman. The Joker sets a plan in motion that may lead to the destruction of all the citizens of Gotham and Batman. Will Batman defeat the Joker or will both die fighting?

  • The Walking Dead

    • The Walking Dead is a tale that involves Rick Grimes a police officer who awakes from a coma amidst a zombie apocalypse. He must find his family and survive. However the most terrifying things they encounter are not the walking dead, but other survivors.
